On-Board Diagnostics (OBD)

Almost all of the automobiles produced today are required, by law, to provide an interface for the connection of diagnostic test equipment. The data transfer on these interfaces follow several standards, but none of them are directly usable by PCs or smart devices.

Using a Scan-Tool device based on the Integrated-Circuit ELM327, I have developed my own PC program (GUI interface) to communicate with the ODB of my car through a serial connection. I am able to request sensors measurements of my car such as the Coolant Temperature, Vehicle speed, Engine RPM, Battery Voltage, obtain trouble codes (and reset them) and probably more ...
